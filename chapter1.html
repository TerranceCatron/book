In a specific area located in the pacific ocean at the moonlit night Quntanium was born, washed up on the shores of New South Wales, Australia. Quntamium doesnt now where he is nor who he came from. All he knows he is from the ocean, staring up in the night sky poundering the beauty of it. Days, weeks, and months have past still living on the shore he washed up on on, Quntanium survives by hunting hunting fish, finding native fruits, and scavage whatever is there. Suddenly a big crash was heard all around the market, screams and shouts raising as the crowd scatter, while some stayed to see the action. The three warriors were standing together, one complaning in a feminine tone "Hey try not to hit so many stands next time, people here don't like us enough already" another warrior in a masculine voice replies "what do you expect me to do let him attack us, gotta strike when they least expect it. The moment was perfect". The last warrior, a female standing over the cursed spirit's body uses a lantern to capture the spirit. Quntanium appering in front of the three asking with stars in his eyes "let me come with you please I want to go on amazing adventures and fight monsters like what yall did, that was so cool". Like a child asking his mother for a toy that peaks his interest he was dying for adventure. The three warriors laughed and denied his request saying he's better off playing sports with the other kids-like. Quntanium with a burning passion in his soul swear an oath that he'll follow them ends of the earth and prove he's ready for adventure.
